Alzheimer's disease is mainly responsible for deleting the most cherished memories of a person in relation to his family, closest friends, favorite activities and favorite places, among others. With advancing disturbance, Alzheimer's patients fail to recognize where they live and try to run away from home. The number of patients is estimated at 35.6 million worldwide. Due to the aging of the global population, these numbers will increase significantly in 2030 and will be 65.7 million in 2050, 115.4 million carriers and two thirds of them in developing countries. We think of a way to find this lost patient to prevent further problems to your health and family.

Alzheimer Track, is a mobile application that uses the QR Code technology to find patients who may be lost in the street due to the memory loss caused by the disease. The patient should wear an Alzheimer Track Bracelet that was previously registered. This unique code will allow any person or institution that find application through the scanner in Alzheimer Track. After scan, some people pre-registered, receive an alarm that a patient was found in the street, with GPS coordinates and telephone contact, allowing the rescue of the person.

The Package and Technologies

The Alzheimer Track is composed with the following components:

  • An beautiful bracelet with QR Code to create an account
  • An website where you can administrate your account and receive informations about QR code activities
  • An Android App to to administrate your account, scan lost people and check o lot of informations and reports
